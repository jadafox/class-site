My Photography Journey

How it all began....

When I first started photography, I was in high school. It wasn't until recenlty that I came to love photography. A couple years ago, I bought my first camera.

It was a Canon T7. This was only the beginning of what was to come.

After I got my camera, I went on Youtube to try and learn everything I could. I knew that over time I would get better, and long story short I did.

I started off taking pictures of nature; mainly plants, trees, grass, etc. Then I started taking pictures of actual people.

This spawned into my love for portrait photography. I have been blessed enough to have taken picuters of my family, my sister graduating college, done soem fashion photoragphy, and my coolest one yet...an engagement shoot!

Never did I think I would see myself doing engagement shoots or anything of that nature. It was a dream come true. Who knows what the future holds. Maybe I'll be a wedding photographer...

What I have learned

I have learned to believe in your passion and it will allow you to work with awesome people and learn an awesome skill.
